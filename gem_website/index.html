Technical Specifications

Under the hood, GEM combines energy-efficient components with dual-power capability.

Power & Battery

  • Capacity: 600mAh Rechargeable Battery
  • Backup Time: 3-4 Hours of active unplugged operation
  • Charging Port: USB Type-C fast charging
  • Charging Time: 1-2 Hours to fully charge
  • Power Source: Supports both USB plug power and battery

Hardware & Connectivity

  • Microcontroller: ESP32 Dual-Core Processor
  • WiFi Capability: 2.4GHz WiFi (802.11 b/g/n)
  • Update Protocol: Wireless OTA Firmware Updates
  • Light Sensor: Calibrated LDR Ambient Light Sensor
  • Touch Sensor: Capacitive Touch Pins
